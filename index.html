RAM: Random Access Mayhem - Bot Overview and Trivia

RAM: Random Access Mayhem is Ultrakill meets Nuclear Throne meets Super Mario Odyssey. RAM is a frantic, high-octane roguelike top-down shooter with no player character. Instead, you play as a GOLEM, a murderous computer virus that can SWAP, swapping their control from one enemy into another. Each of RAM's 8 core robots function both as challenging enemies and player-controllable hosts, with movesets that you fight as and against in a variety of intense combat arenas. But RAM's enemies can be difficult to get a sense of how to properly play them, and what synergies do or don't exist. So I made a website all about RAM's core cast of badass bots!
