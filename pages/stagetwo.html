Tachi

"Nekkyo no shunkan to ora"

Primary-Fire: Art of the Blade. Use your gravity pylons to telekinetically control a floating sword that spins, deflects projectiles, and deals light damage and knockback on contact. Hold Primary-Fire to prime the sword, and release to slingshot it away from the cursor at enemies, dealing moderate damage, knockback, and stun. Impaled enemies are also slowed heavily until the sword is jostled loose by impact force, firing it out as a projectile.

Secondary-Fire: CLIPPED-WING-BUTTERFLY-INSTANT-DEATH-BLUESCREEN CRESENT. Enter KILL MODE, sheathing your sword to dash around at high speeds. Attacking unleashes a C.W.B.I.D.B.S.C., INSTANTLY KILLING anything hit and shattering your sword.

SPECIAL: Blade Master - Killing enemies with swords embedded in them with C.W.B.I.D.B.S.C. re-primes your sheath, allowing you to continue KILL MODE. Duration can be extended as long as there are embedded enemies to kill.

Best Upgrades:

  • Induction Barrel - Sets your pellets on fire, converting them into piercing flames!

  • Stacked Shells - Surely more shells can fit in the gun, right? Gain an extra barrel (or three), and unload all of them at once!

  • Ricochet Shells - Bullets bounce off walls. What kind of idiot thinks bullets don't bounce off walls?

Trivia:

  • According to Word of God from the RAM 1.0 devlog, the gimmick of embedding swords in enemies was inspired by a scene from Chainsaw Man.
  • The Forbidden Oathblade weapon line from the Terraria Calamity Mod was based off of Tachi. The Brainstorm Update was delayed because many devs were busy playing RAM, which is why there's a decent bit of crossover content.
  • Never bring a plasma gun to a plasma sword fight.
  • Amongst Tachis, the art of wielding multiple blades at once is considered a heresy, and a skill thought lost to time. However, at least one strange and eccentric Tachi has perfected the domain of the Fractured Mind. Fuhuhu~
  • The unused song Tachi Fortress was intended for Stage 2's second half. It was removed for being too hype and badass. It has been included on this website for your listening pleasure
